== Chief Complaint

A 68-year-old male presents to the emergency department with progressive
dyspnea on exertion over the past two weeks, accompanied by bilateral
lower extremity edema.

== Assessment

Physical examination and echocardiographic findings are consistent with
k:HeartFailure[] secondary to severe k:AorticStenosis[].

The patient's history is significant for k:TypeTwoDiabetesMellitus[]
diagnosed 12 years ago, currently managed with metformin and
empagliflozin. Renal function has been declining over the past 3 years,
meeting criteria for stage 3b k:ChronicKidneyDisease[CKD].

== Differential Diagnosis

The acute worsening of k:HeartFailure[congestive heart failure] must be
evaluated in the context of the known k:AorticStenosis[]. Given the
patient's recent immobilization, k:PulmonaryEmbolism[] should also be
excluded. The risk of k:AcuteMyocardialInfarction[acute MI] is elevated
given the hemodynamic stress imposed by the valvular disease.

== Plan

. Obtain CT pulmonary angiography to rule out k:PulmonaryEmbolism[]
. Serial troponins to evaluate for k:AcuteMyocardialInfarction[]
. Optimize k:HeartFailure[] management with IV diuresis
. Nephrology consultation for k:ChronicKidneyDisease[] staging
  and contrast risk assessment
. Cardiothoracic surgery consultation regarding intervention
  for k:AorticStenosis[]
